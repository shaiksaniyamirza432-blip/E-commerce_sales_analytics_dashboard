# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and visualize key business metrics from e-commerce sales data. This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights, helping businesses monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales, profit, and order performance.
- Monitor monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales across product categories.
- Evaluate regional sales contributions.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyze payment method preferences.
- Create an interactive and business-focused dashboard.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Sales:** 34.05M
- **Total Profit:** 6.05M
- **Total Orders:** 2.5K

### Visualizations Included
- Sales by Category
- Sales Trend Over Time
- Sales by Region
- Profit Performance Gauge
- Orders by Category (Treemap)
- Orders by Payment Method

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Fashion generated the highest sales among all categories.
- The West region contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Credit Card emerged as the most preferred payment method.
- Monthly sales showed consistent performance throughout the year.
- The business achieved a strong profit of 6.05M from total sales of 34.05M.
